~ TIFA ~

Origin

The tifa originates from the eastern islands of Indonesia, mainly Maluku and Papua.

Technique

Played with hands to produce deep, rhythmic beats that drive traditional songs.

Symbolism

Represents the heartbeat of the community. A rhythm connecting people and nature.

Function

Traditionally used in dances, ceremonies, and storytelling performances.

Ensemble

Often paired with vocals, flutes, or dances to accompany cultural performances.

Material

Usually made from hollowed wood covered with animal skin on one side.
