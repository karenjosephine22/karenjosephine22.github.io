~ ANGKLUNG ~

Origin

The Angklung comes from West Java, Indonesia, known for its spirit of unity and cooperation.

How It Works

Each Angklung plays a single note. When shaken together, they form melodies, symbolizing teamwork and harmony.

Cultural Meaning

It represents harmony, balance, and togetherness. Often performed in groups to express unity.

Sound & Emotion

Its bright, rhythmic tone brings joy and connection in ceremonies and performances.

Fun Fact

UNESCO recognized the Angklung as a Masterpiece of the Intangible Heritage of Humanity in 2010.

Material

Made from carefully cut bamboo tubes, each tuned to a specific pitch and mounted on a frame.
