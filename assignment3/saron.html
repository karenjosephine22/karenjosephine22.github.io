~ SARON ~

Origin

Rebana is a traditional frame drum found across Indonesia, especially in Java and Sumatra.

Technique

Played by hand, producing different tones based on striking areas and finger pressure.

Symbolism

Represents community harmony and rhythmic unity in Muslim cultural traditions.

Function

Commonly used in religious ceremonies, weddings, and traditional performances.

Ensemble

Often performed in groups, creating layered rhythms with other percussion instruments.

Material

Made from wood and goatskin, stretched tightly over the frame for a resonant sound.
