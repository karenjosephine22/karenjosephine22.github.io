~~~~Handheld Windmill Fan ~~~~

Tools and Materials

  • Cardstock (15×15 cm)
  • Decorative paper / paint
  • Wooden skewer / handle
  • Small bead / washer
  • Brass brad / push pin
  • Scissors
  • Glue / hot glue gun
  • Ruler, Pencil, Eraser

~ Step-by-step ~

(1) Cut a sheet of cardstock to a clean square (e.g. 15×15 cm).
(2) Mark the exact center with a pencil.
(3) From each corner, draw a diagonal line toward the center and cut, stopping around half the length from the center.
(4) Fold every other corner point toward the center to form the pinwheel shape.
(5) Stack folded points, make a circle using the sheet leftovers and make a hole in the middle.
(6) Place a bead/washer, and push the brad through the center.
(7) Insert the brad into your skewer/dowel and make sure it spins freely.
(8) Adjust tightness with a washer/glue if needed.
(9) Blow gently to test spin and decorate the handle. Done!
